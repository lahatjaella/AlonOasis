Welcome to Alon Oasis

Where Waves of Tranquility Meet Luxurious Comfort

At Alon Oasis, we embrace the natural beauty and serenity of the Philippines. The name "Alon" means "waves" in Tagalog, symbolizing the rhythmic, calming influence of the ocean and the seamless flow of nature. Our hotel is a sanctuary where the gentle waves of comfort and luxury meet the lush, tranquil surroundings, creating a perfect retreat in the heart of Manila.
